Our Services

Locker Rental

Need a safe place to store your books, tools, or project parts? Rent a locker through the club for the semester at an affordable rate. Conveniently located near our club room.

Club Room Amenities

Our club room is your space to study, hang out, and collaborate. Enjoy free Wi-Fi, whiteboards, project workbenches, and a microwave for those late nights on campus.

Textbook Drop-Off & Take Out

We run a community textbook shelf — drop off textbooks you don’t need and pick up ones you do. It’s free, sustainable, and keeps resources circulating among our members.
